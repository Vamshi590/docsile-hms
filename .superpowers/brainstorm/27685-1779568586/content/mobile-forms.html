Forms & Multi-Step Wizards on Mobile

Your app has large multi-step forms (patient registration, inpatient admission, prescription, billing). How should they behave on mobile?

A

Single Column, Full Width

All form fields stack into one column on mobile. Labels above inputs. Each step takes up the full screen width. Clean, easy to type on phone keyboard.

Two Columns on Tablet, One on Phone

Tablet (768px+) keeps a 2-column layout for efficiency. Phone gets single column. Best of both worlds — tablet users see more at once, phone users get easy typing.

One Question Per Screen (Wizard Style)

Each field or small group gets its own screen on mobile. Very phone-friendly — big touch targets, no scrolling. But many more taps to complete the form. Better for simple forms, awkward for complex ones.
